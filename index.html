About Me

I'm a certified accessibility tester with 6+ years of experience helping organisations build inclusive digital products. My work sits at the intersection of technical testing, user empathy, and standards compliance.

I've partnered with government agencies, healthcare platforms, and e-commerce companies to audit, remediate, and integrate accessibility into every stage of the development lifecycle — not just at the finish line.

  • 200+ Audits completed
  • 40+ Client teams trained
  • 98% WCAG pass rate

Skills & Expertise

A full toolkit for finding, fixing, and preventing accessibility issues.

  • Manual Testing

    Keyboard navigation, screen reader testing with NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver, and TalkBack across real devices.

  • Automated Auditing

    Axe-core, Lighthouse, Deque WorldSpace, and Pa11y integrated into CI/CD pipelines for continuous testing.

  • WCAG Compliance

    Deep expertise in WCAG 2.0 through 2.2, Section 508, EN 301 549, and ADA digital requirements.

  • User Research

    Moderated usability sessions with disabled users. Lived-experience insight that automated tools can't catch.

  • Reporting & Remediation

    Clear, prioritised VPAT reports and developer-ready remediation guides in plain language.

  • Team Training

    Workshops and onboarding programs for designers, developers, and QA teams on building accessible-first.
