About Me

I'm a certified accessibility tester with 6+ years of experience helping organisations build inclusive digital products. My work sits at the intersection of technical testing, user empathy, and standards compliance.

I've partnered with government agencies, healthcare platforms, and e-commerce companies to audit, remediate, and integrate accessibility into every stage of the development lifecycle — not just at the finish line.

  • 200+ Audits completed
  • 40+ Client teams trained
  • 98% WCAG pass rate
